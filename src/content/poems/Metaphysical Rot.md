# Metaphysical Rot
Bullshit
Pathetic rubbish
Phlegm and spit
Inconsequential grot
Throat clearings and farts

That’s what all of this is
Beauty, love, and truth 
This metaphysical rot
Disguised in flesh and worse
In words

Gunk of blight
Squelching offal, fetid
Silk draped, perfumed
Pus and snot

Splattered as meaning
Directions
Doctrines 
Altars and gods

Reeking festering
Sickly stink
Drenched in blood
Tombstones 

That’s your life
Reality
Meaning of all words
