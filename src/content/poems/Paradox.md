# Paradox
I write
I think

But never has a self 
Ever written, spoken, or heard
A single word

And never but a self
Has ever written, spoken, or heard
A single word

Why

Because neither the self —
Nor the word spoken or heard —
Exist

And only the self
Has ever spoken, written, or heard
Any single word
