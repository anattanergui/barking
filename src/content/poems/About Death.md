# About Death
It’s the mind 
That dies
First 

Then, the body
Takes a while — to die
But before that

Body also dies
In the eyes of the mind

And in the sightless eye
That never existed —
Of the body

Seeing that 
The body 
Is all that’s ever born

And that it is
That which knows
No death
