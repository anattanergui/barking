# The Sacrament
One can only ever 
Give — truly give —
What one has, in actuality
Factually

The only thing — substance —
That one has, is one’s own
To give — is body

Body is the only means 
And form — of connection —
Of giving 
Between humans

Body is the only thing
That can be given — truly given —
Without ever 
Becoming divided, diminished
Or made un-whole
In its giving

It is the only thing — ever —
Fully able to be given
That can never 
Be made unholy — 
Never be taken, in its giving

It is the only sacrament
Altar, church
And the sacred
Itself

Taking of body 
By force 
Is sacrilege, desecration
Of the sacred 

But never —
Its negation, never its violation
That is only possible
Through annihilation
Of the body
