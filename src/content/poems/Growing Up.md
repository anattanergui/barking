# Growing Up
What’s growing up
But seeing —
And then seeing through
The impossibilities 

As standing on a rooftop
Looking at the ground below
Knowing the impossibility
Of escaping the fall

And then 
Again —
Looking down
And jumping anyway —

Now 
Not to escape the fall
But to embrace 
The ground below

Knowing —
That ceaseless, interminable knowing
Remains —
Through the fall, smashing into the ground 
Untouched

What’s growing up 
But seeing 
And seeing through impossibilities
Walking and striving
Jumping and breaking
Through them all — 

Standing —
Untouched
