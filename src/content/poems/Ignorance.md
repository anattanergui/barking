# Ignorance
Agents of organised religions —
Theologians, evangelists
Have the impulse

To look outside 
And to draw the other 
Away from himself

Religions themselves —
Fragments of utterances
Narratives and texts
Have no such impulse

Any string of words —
Traced from within
From the place where words originate —
Only ever points 
Inward

God of the organised religions
Is the plaque 
Upon the institutional walls

Stolen from — relinquished by —
Each follower 
Sold back to him

That he can once again —
Seek as a treasure

Or see as the price — 
Of ignorance
