# What Or Who
What are the options 
When you find yourself 
Caught in physicality —
Be it on earth, water or air 

As what or who 
Can you talk about freedom 
When you find yourself caught 
To begin with 

As what or who 
Can you talk about being caught 
When the one caught 
Is never found
