# Condensation
That woman sitting before me
Smiling and looking —
Big bust
Hands on the table
Open 

Thoughts — unholy —
Arise in my mind
Linger a while 
And disperse

Leaving the air of my mind
Moist and humid

Could she see those thoughts
Through the condensation
In my eyes
When I answered her —
About something or the other
Unrelated —

I didn’t say what I thought
She wouldn’t have said
What she thought

Instead, we talked about 
Future and past —
Not about what rose
And dispersed 
What was felt and sensed
By each of us

Not about affection 
Attraction 
That tinge 
Of loss

I felt 
For her

Did we not communicate
All that — without speaking —
Through our eyes meeting
Breathing
While speaking 
About other things

If we didn’t 
How’d I know 
What I thought
Felt 
And sensed

The air between us
Filled with love —
As she sat there
Unhidden 
Before me
