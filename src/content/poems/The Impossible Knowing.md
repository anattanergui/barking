# The Impossible Knowing
Life means self
Self means life
No self, no life 
No life, no self

There is no life
And there is no self
Yet it is impossible
Not to feel alive
And not to feel like oneself

But it is possible to know
That there is no life
And no self

It is impossible 
Not to see, not to feel, not to think
It is possible to know
That the seen, the felt and the thought
Do not exist
