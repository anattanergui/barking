# I Am That
What could I wish, knowing

That I am
As The Buddha Is
As God, The All Knower

As The Truth, Eternal
The Absolute, Immovable
The Supreme, Alone

What could I wish, knowing
That Nothing Is
But I
That my gaze The Gaze
My hearing The Listening
My feeling The Touch
Of The Only God

Without possessing
I possess all
Without asking
I give all

What am I to ask
From the Heaven
And this World
