# Constitution
Money 
Like a drop of water 
On top of a mountain flows
Downward, to become 
A tiny stream

Naturally moving toward
The valley, to become
A pond, or a river
Continuing, flowing
Toward the ocean

Each larger 
Body of water — rich
Is made, constituted by
Drops of water — poor

Once seen
The folly ends

The flow reverses
