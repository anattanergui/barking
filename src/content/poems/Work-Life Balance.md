# Work-Life Balance
In context of
Work-life balance 

The hardest part 
Is letting go 

Of past identities 
Of more — at the cost

Of sufficiency —
That becomes 

Obvious
On seeing

That living 
Is already work

And working 
To live 
To make a living

Is false
