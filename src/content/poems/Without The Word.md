# Without The Word
Words are the building blocks
Of thought

Thoughts are the building blocks
Of the universe

The universe is the body of God
God is the word of man

Man is the word of man
Standing between him and himself —

Seeing him, himself, and all the seen
As but 
Itself

Without it in between
None is left to know the known —
Not even
Silence
Stillness
Or unknowing
