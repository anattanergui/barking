# Products And Services
What can the body
Ever want
From another body

Comfort
Necessities for daily living
Perhaps, even sympathy —
A shoulder
A listening ear
A touch

But really — 
What can I — this body —
Ever want from another 
Other than
Goods and postures

Is there anything more — 
Than products and services
That make my life
Comfortable
Make my living 
Possible

The other’s death does not concern me
So long as it does not 
Disrupt my comfort
Or threaten
My living 

And just for that — 
There’s an entire world
Of relationships 
Civilisations, wars and fear

Just for the needs
Of the body —
The body whose fate

Is doomed
