# Impossibility
It is impossible to explain
Creation
In any rational way 

As soon as we begin
To speak about the created or creation —
We have already failed 

Be it through science, religion
Myth or metaphor —
No explanation or understanding 
Is possible

And the reason for all this —
Is simple
The created and the creation
Exist in time

And time —
Does not exist

The certainty
And the frameworks 
To explain the existence —

Of that which is founded
On non-existent —

Are the definitions 
Of conceit, ignorance 
And vanity
