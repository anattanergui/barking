# About Writing
Writing is —
Like exhibitionism
Stripping to see —
What wouldn’t have been seen
Couldn’t be seen
Were it not for writing 

It’s like 
Coprophilia —
A fascination with 
Re-arranging, re-writing
Expulsions
Waste products

It’s like 
Necrophilia —
Exhuming, over and over
Each time
Finding a new nakedness
The sight of yet another cadaver
Of oneself

And our revulsion
Toward these acts
Is our very affinity
For sanitised, soft porn —
A safeguard
Against our own 
Self-cannibalisation
