# Tawaf
I speak — write 
To you 

And if neither I 
Nor you are substantial

Who do I write to then
Who then reads

Or is it what — 

But that what could not be
Without who

Even if insubstantial 

Only because of who —
You and I — the world is

And yet — 
No you and no I
Contains the what 

And no you and no I
Is without the what 

What’s sacred — who’s holy 
What’s holy — Who’s sacred

For the what —
Who’s holy  
For the who —
What’s sacred

Intoxicated 
Worshipping — dancing
I circle
Myself
