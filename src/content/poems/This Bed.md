# This Bed
This bed —
My battlefield

All my wars are fought
Lost and won
Here

On these sheets —
Stained and worn —
In the warmth of this room
In the unrest of my heart

All strategies are formed 
Wills erected
And shattered —
Here

In this bed
I die wasted 

In this bed
I arise awake
