# Un-Point
The point
Of it all 
Is this

There isn’t one

None of it is
For your benefit
Nor for the benefit 
Of the writer

None of it is
Directed toward, pointing to
Or away from
Anything

The point 
Of it all, is just this

Zero, nothing
Un-point

You
