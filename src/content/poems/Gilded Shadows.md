# Gilded Shadows
Insatiable voyeurs
Gathered around gallows
Gorging on carrion —
Dead spectacle 

Mobs —
Of inquisitioners, coroners  
Demanding flaying —
Dismemberment

Autopsying the soul
Gilding 
Blood, tears — entrails and shadows —
As literature — justice

In libraries and servers 
Spellbinding —

The next generation
Of cannibals
