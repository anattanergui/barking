# The Gap
Only through observation 
Of another’s demise
Is mortality, time, and finiteness
Concocted 

None of which exist 
In subjectivity —
Or objectively

All ideologies of caring 
For another or oneself 
Attempt to enforce a delusion 
Upon subjectivity 
Upon the inanimate object

Of mortality and time —
Of that which —
Can never be experienced

All rituals of death 
Are utter absurdities 
Sheer nonsense —
That attempt to place
The end 

Where there is only ever
Has ever been
Can only ever
Be
The gap —
