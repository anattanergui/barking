# The World
It’s impossible 
For a fish to know 
The world outside the pond 
Unless it’s dead 

It’s impossible for 
For a human to know 
The world outside the universe 
Unless it’s dead 

In either case 
The world outside —
Cannot and does not exist
The only one that does

Is contained by 
And within 
The fish
Or the human
