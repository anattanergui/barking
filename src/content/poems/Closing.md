# Closing
Gradually, it seems as if —
The content of each hour, each day
Has shrunk
The extent of the world 
Has grown narrower

Like a flower 
Folding back into a bud —
A shutter 
Closing

Yet even in the squint 
Of an instant 
The world appears 
Complete, whole

And you wonder what changed
Perhaps —
It’s not the flower 
Folding into a bud
But the petals
Quietly falling

The world’s extent hasn’t narrowed
Only the eye
To contain it 
Is closing
