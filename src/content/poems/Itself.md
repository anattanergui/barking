# Itself
Breathing
This breath, unseen — being pulled
Going in — and then
Out —
The very next instant

Pulled in
Again —

Endlessly — but not endlessly —

Breath — air

As water for fish 
Fish —
Looking 

At itself
Breathing 

Pulling in 
Out 

Stones, algae, water
Elements
Earth, sky
Breath

Itself

Same 
All same

Churn

Samsara

Dark
Silent
Empty

Reality
