# Mania
The vine —
Teeming with flowers
Burdened by leaves —
Branches surging
Sweeping 
Clinging to the wall
Dangling 
Swaying in the air

Ground blanketed
With scattered flowers
Air thick
With scent

Such frenzy —
Call the police
The medics
Restrain the vine
Detain it
Pump it
With antipsychotics
Mood stabilisers
