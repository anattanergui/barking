# Dead Vibrations
Family is
The smallest structural unit
Of society
Tribe, or nation

Market is
The tissue —
That connects and coheres
The unit
Together

In itself neither alive
Nor dead
The expanding product
Sprawl 
Extracting life
From each organism
In a unit

Harvested excretions
Howls, shouts
Sound’s capacity
To reach — beyond sight
The germ, force
Organising principle 
Of market

From loudspeakers
To networks — invisible
Dead vibrations
Occupying the entirety 
Of space 
Between forms — pushing each 
Further apart

In order
To hold each
Together
