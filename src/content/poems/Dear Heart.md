# Dear Heart
O dear heart —

Beat slowly
Slowly, slowly 

Nowhere to arrive —
Rushing, galloping

Reaching beyond 
This cage

Steeped in past
Without the future
Rest —
Dear heart

Blanketed 
Embraced
Within the chest —

Hum gently
Beat slowly
