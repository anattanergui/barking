# Khudi
In whose selfhood I was
Before arriving as self —
My mother that is

In whose selfhood I will be
After departing as self —
My mother that is

In whose selfhood I am
While being a self —
My mother that is

My mother —
Eternal 
God

My mother and I
Never — were ever —
Apart
