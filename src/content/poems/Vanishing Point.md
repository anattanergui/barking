# Vanishing Point
What’s a play
In which — about which —
You cannot tell

Whether you are the audience
The play, theatre 
Or the player 

And even if there really is
A play  

And when the you
That cannot tell
Is nowhere found

What becomes, then
Of not knowing
What’s not known
