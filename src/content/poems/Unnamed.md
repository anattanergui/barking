# Unnamed
Having forgotten
Who or what I am supposed to be
I wander 
Days and nights
Seeing each thing — place —
For the first time

Not knowing
The names of what’s seen
Heard or felt

Untouched 
By rightness and wrongness
I sit, breathe
And write —

To sanctify
Being
