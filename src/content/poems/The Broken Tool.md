# The Broken Tool
You can never say a single thing
About truth, love
Beauty
Or being

Without leaving your —
Your unique fingerprints
On that thing 
That isn’t a thing at all

And clearly 
To say even that 
Is a fingerprint

To say that it is 
A thing 
Factual, objective or actual 
Is another fingerprint

Speaking 
Is destined to fail
From its first utterance
If it’s meant to articulate
Point at or show 

The metaphysical ground
Essence, nature
Truth or reality
Of existence

And speaking —
Despite its utter inadequacy 
Is the only mechanism
That exists 

To point to 
Show or articulate
What exists  

Beyond its reach
Uncontaminated 
By fingerprints

Knowing this —
Silence is no greater teaching 
Has no greater claim —
To whatever it's pointing at
Showing or representing

Than speech
