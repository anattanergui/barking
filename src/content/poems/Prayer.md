# Prayer
Lord —

Is it I 
Who forgets you —
Or you
Who forgets me

In moments
Of trifling fixations —
Mapping
Plotting
Planning —

I know 
I have it all
Yet this form 
Grasps 
Holds —

Finding nothing
Returns to you

I know 
I have nothing at all
Yet this form
Pursues
Craves —

Finding nothing
Returns to you

Lord —
Do I play
This game with you

Or do you
Play this game 
With me
