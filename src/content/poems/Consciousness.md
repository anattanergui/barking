# Consciousness
In every embrace of the day
In the depths of the night
Through every touch
Amidst each sense
In the depths of the mind
In the vastness of the world —

Starved or satiated
On the mountaintop
In the marketplace
Through each of your step
Pangs of pain
During each breath —

From the first
To the last

In the final analysis
You are
All alone

No one else but you
In the theatre
Of your dreams
