# I Am
Every fucker knows

I am

What most don’t know
Is that 
Everything else they know
Other than I am

Is what they don’t know

And also —
What most don’t know
Is that 

It’s not them
That knows

I am

Nor is it them
That doesn’t know 
That it’s them

That doesn’t know

I am
