# Fuck The Dependable
Fuck all the metaphors
The clichés
Fuck them all  

What’s there
What can one depend on —
What can be depended on  

No friend
No well-wisher
No mother or father
No wife or lover
No son or daughter
No brother or sister  

Who can be depended on
What can be depended on  
That’s what I want to know  

And that is precisely the problem  
There is something that is scared —
That wants to hold on to something

A lasting anchor
A steady ground
A truth that won’t break

But the very thing that is scared
That is seeking the dependable —
Is non-existent  

The dependable  
That which is seeking the dependable —
Isn’t there

That which is in search of the dependable —
Is fictitious
The character in the movie
Is in search of something dependable  

But the character is the problem
As long as there is character —
There is search  

Who am I then  
I cannot possibly be 
All the opinions I seek
Of others —
All the judgements
That others have placed on me

Where can those judgements be placed
Where can those opinions be held  

On fictional stage of mind —
Made with wisps of thoughts

Life appears
Sees itself 
In its own light  —

And then what
Does it  vanish
In that same light   

I can never find out
Who or what or where I am  

Why the fuck must I find it out
Who or what in me wishes to  
From where did I get all these ideas  

What the fuck  

Nonduality —
Fuck nonduality

Damn the Second
The First and the Third
And the Sixth and Last
And the Seventh —
Damn them all  

Fuck all of these teachers 
And gurus 
And their words and teachings  

Fuck them all  

What words do I have 
Once all the words 
Have been purged out of my system  

Purge it all
