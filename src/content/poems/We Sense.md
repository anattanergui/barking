# We Sense
We think
That we think

We sense
Not think

We see
And feel —
Mostly

What is sensed
Is
What
Is
Mind

Is
World
Is
I, self

Soul
Spirit
Awareness
Consciousness

And in all this —
There’s
No me
No I
No self

No soul
No spirit

No awareness
No consciousness

Ever found
