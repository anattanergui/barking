# Name And Form
Your worldly name
Is not yours
Nor your name
Only 
A pseudonym

Your body
Is not yours
Nor your body

Only a sack —
Fragments
Of the world

These sounds, words
Meanings —
Whooshes of wind
Sounds of sea
Crashing

How can any
Of it be yours
When you are

The seeing
Being

Names
And forms
