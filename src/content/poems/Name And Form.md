# Name And Form
Your worldly name
Is not yours
Nor your name
Only 
A pseudonym

Your body
Is not yours
Nor your body

Only a sackful —
Of fragments 
Of the world

These sounds, words
Meanings —
Whooshes of wind
Sounds of the sea
Crashing

How can any
Of this be yours 
When you are

The eye
Being —

At names
Forms
