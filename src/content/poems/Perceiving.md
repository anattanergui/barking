# Perceiving
Doing 
One thing in service
Of another 

Waiting
For this doing, deed
To bear fruits

Taking this step
Writing this word
For the arrival
Of the next

This inhalation 
For
The exhalation 
Before
The inhalation
After

What is time 
But movement 
Perceived

No escape
From perception

Perceiving 
Is movement

That is where 
Life lives
That is where
Sorrow lives
