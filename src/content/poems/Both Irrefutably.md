# Both Irrefutably
Individual sovereignty and no self
Appear to be
Mutually exclusive
Even counter-cancelling

Yet both are irrefutably 
Simultaneously —
Factual and self-verifiable

And both —
Are the most subverted
Inverted 
Truths —

For societies
To be
