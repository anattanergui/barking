# In My Bed
In my bed —
Never a second late
Or an instant too soon —
Universe comes 
Unbidden, ready 
Without a particle missing

What else could be sought
Facing her —
In a blink — beholding
The virgin dazzle
Of her infinity

Tracelessly, I walk
On the unmapped horizons —
Neither following, nor leading
Without ever moving
In any direction

Before all beginnings
After all the ends
In the heavens
And all hells —
I encounter no one
But myself
