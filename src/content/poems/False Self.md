# False Self
Deflated —
By stripping in public 
Selling what — really —
Fragments of experience — 
A few assertions 
Clarifications
Deliberations
Reflections
Constructed thoughts

A face, a name, borrowed titles —
what, really —
A promise: 
Arrive at 9 AM
Stay until 5 PM
Five days a week 
Week after week 
Follow procedures 
Sit, talk, write 

For some money 
For some wages —
But before that:
Perform a public stripping 
A ritual dance
Woo, assert, convince, clarify —
So they will buy you
Buy you

They buy your precious little life
Your entire waking day:
Your attention, your focus
Your body and your mind
Your submission 
Your agreement 

Why — O man —
Why must you
Be so cheap
So cheap
