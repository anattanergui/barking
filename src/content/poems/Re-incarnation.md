# Re-incarnation
Midnight
Insomnia, pain
End of life

Before
The perceiving, the sensing 
Shapes into form

Before
The form, thought, the words
Are reckoned

What could, would
Be amiss
Wrong

This 
Only this
Capacity 

To comprehend at all
To sense, to perceive at all
To be, in the first place

Without this
No wrong is possible
All wrong is possible 
All can be wrong

The moment
The instant
Of incarnation
