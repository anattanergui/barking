You cannot buy groceries
With theories
Or ideas — nor walk into a shop
As disembodied consciousness

You cannot even think
About groceries
Without body

Cannot breathe
Do not exist 
Are not real —
As consciousness without body

You do not remain 
Without body 
To say consciousness
Exists — or is real

More so — than body

There is no more —
No less
No side can ever 
Ever 
Be said to be more 

No side has ever —
Nor can ever be — experienced
Known, spoken of
Conceived, or thought 
Without the other
