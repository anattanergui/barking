# Unlike Spider
A spider 
That keeps getting stuck
Caught, trapped
In its own silk

Keeps weaving
Around its own legs
Might be called
Deficient
Developmentally arrested
Perhaps hapless

What might humans
Constructing, propping up
Reifying 
Institutions — while being caught
Trapped, and crushed
By them, be called

Given that humans
Unlike spider
Can see the trap

Developmentally arrested
Or complicit
Conceited
