# Because It Could
A poem 

Like work of art
Piece of music
Building, monument, rocket
Carved stick

Each word, sentence, thought
Within is like
Nuts and bolts
Bricks, mortar
Brushstrokes and colours —
Each singular touch
On an instrument

All coming together
Into 
A poem

A poem exists
For its own sake
First, and foremost

It exists simply because
It can exist
Because
It could exist

Like life
With its unimaginable, astounding
Immense complexity 
Diversity and depth
Exists
Because
It could
Because it does

Writers
Builders, composers, artists
May think
That their work
Exists for other reasons

Ten thousand reasons 
A bridge to bridge the gap
Stick to aid the walk
Book to teach 
Music, painting, poems
To inspire
Evoke wonder

Yet first —
Each touch 
Lifting a brick
Holding a brush
A pen 
Or an instrument 
Happens —

Happened 
Because it could
Because it did

That’s why I write
That’s why you read
