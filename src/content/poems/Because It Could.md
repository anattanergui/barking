# Because It Could
A poem 

Like a work of art
A piece of music
Building, monument, rocket
Carved stick

Each word, sentence, thought
Within it is like
Nuts and bolts
Bricks and mortar
Brushstrokes and colours —
Each singular touch on an instrument

All coming together
Into 
A poem

The poem
Exists for its own sake
First and foremost

It exists simply because
It can exist
Because
It could exist

Like life
With its unimaginable, astounding
Immense complexity —
Diversity and depth
Exists
Simply because
It could
Because it does

The writers
Builders, composers
And artists
May think
That their work
Exists for other reasons
Ten thousand reasons —
A bridge to bridge the gap
Stick to aid the walk
A book to teach 
Music, painting, poems
To inspire
Evoke wonder

Yet
First and foremost
Each touch —
Lifting a brick
Holding a brush
A pen — or an instrument 

Happens — 
Happened
Because it could
Because it did

That’s why I write
That’s why you read
