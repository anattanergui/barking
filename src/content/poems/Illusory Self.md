# Illusory Self
Illusory self is narration —
No narration
No illusory self

But not all narration —
Is illusory self

Illusory self
Is narration
Comprehended and believed
By no-self —

Of the narrator 
Or of the listener
Both —
No-self

Illusory self, then
Is the capacity 
Of no-self 

To cloak itself 
In narration —

Dis-identification from illusory self
Is non-action because
No-self exists 
To act

And no-self remains —
To believe — even comprehend —
The cloak of narration 
The so-called illusory self 

Or to live, recognise, or comprehend
The so-called freedom
From illusory self
