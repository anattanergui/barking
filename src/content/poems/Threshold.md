# Threshold
Hear, O watcher —
Listener
Seer 
Of this world
And of yourself 

Through this body —
Set down
Still
Lying in bed

Sight
Of the ceiling
Wall
Window

Sensations faded
Breath slowed
Sounds
Feelings
Grief and pain
Memories
And hopes
Snuffed 
In silence 

Dissolving
Setting free
The binds 
Of time
Of this world 

Drop the world
Just here
Nothing to hold
To touch  
No more waiting
To begin
To end

Let life
Merge 
With death 

Both ceasing
