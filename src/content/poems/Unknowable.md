# Unknowable
Is there something I’m missing
You wonder
Is there another place
Another state
Some way to be
Some depth or further beyond
You have missed

You know that you thought
Of a wall 
Beyond which 
You could not see
Could not go
But there too
No wall was found —
Only you

Your own being
Turned out to be the wall
You couldn’t reach beyond

And you wonder
If that is the line
The ground —
Or if there is
Nothing and
Nought
Beyond
