# Becoming Unself
Strange that

You come into your own —
Become yourself
Singular, unrepeatable
Unmistakably, irreproducibly
Yourself —

After 

Much after —
Deeply and thoroughly seeing
Unquestionably realising

That there is not
Never was nor could be —
Either a you
Or a self
