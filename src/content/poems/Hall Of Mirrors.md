# Hall Of Mirrors
Do you really want to speak —
The final truth

Can you even speak 
Your own name
Or speak of what you see
Hear, touch, and feel —
And know that to be
True

What can you speak 
Or say
That would be true
The final truth

Is there anything at all

I —

Even that — is that true
Or just a sound

Who designated 
Gave this duty, burden, obligation —
This responsibility —
To this little creature
This little biped —

The authority to speak
The final —
Or any — truth

Nonsense
Vanity
Conceit

And yes — of course —
It gave itself 
These obligations, duties
Responsibilities and designations

Who else

It
Gave itself the title
I

And called that —
The final truth

What else could it call 
The final truth
But the title
It gave itself

I

And then —
It creates a God

And that — 
It also calls I

But —

Think

What would you do

What possible option
Would you have — 
Could you have — 
But this —

If you
Found yourself 
In

This situation —

Of being — 
This
Biped

Or just
Being —

In 

A hall of mirrors
