# Daimon
There is such a thing —

Though you may call it
By another name —
Dharma, integrity
Soul —
Some inner demon
Even love

It says something like:

Whatever the cost 

And something like: 

This — and nothing else

It is like feet
That would not —
Could not —
Leave the ground
They stand on

Even when the ground
Scorches 
Tears 

Even when your own hands
And head 
Try to lift them off —
Just to sit back
Stretch
Lie down

It’s not something 
You can do
Or not do

It’s something 
You watch
Happen
