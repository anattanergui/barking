# The Wall Wins
What sensitivity
Can possibly be known
Felt or experienced

By a sensitive sentience
Toward
A wall 

Impenetrably cloaking
Wapped around
Another sentience

Frustration
Anger — even hatred —

At best
Indifference
Walking away
Distance

But never 
Compassion
Be known
Felt, experienced

Enabled
For the wall

The wall wins
