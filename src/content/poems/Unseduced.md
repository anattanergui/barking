# Unseduced
Stopping seeing distortion
Does not mean stopping seeing —
It means seeing undistorted
Disillusionment —

An eyes-open view
Without layers of emotions
Melancholy, nostalgia, sentimentality
Borrowed meaning

Like coming to see —
That a family member —
Spouse, sibling, child or parent
Someone you spent an entire lifetime with

With whom you made efforts —
To be known, seen, understood
Understands you the least —

Seeing that the efforts —
Expectation, hope, desire — and grief — 
When expectations are unmet

Were born out of assigning words —
Prepackaged with emotions

To something like a house animal —
That cohabited, shared the same ground
And walls —
Of a house with you 

Seeing your dearest family member —
Unseduced by the sentiment
Of dearest and family
Is stopping seeing distortion —
Disillusionment

That no society 
No culture and no religion
Promotes 

Perhaps because
Another name for disillusionment
Is enlightenment
