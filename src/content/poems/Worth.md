# Worth
What’s the price
Of a lifetime
From birth to old age

Of success, status — grief and loss
Beauty, joy
And tears

What’s the price
Of a cup —
Filled, emptied, refilled
Emptied again

Sitting — walking —
Made of value
Without worth

Costing —
Lifetime
