# Worth
What’s the worth of a man’s life
After all the years, from birth to old age
After experiencing all that life can give —
Success and failure, grief and loss, status
Knowledge, love, beauty, mercy, joy
And tears

What’s the worth of such a man’s life
Is it worth more than the life
Of an ant, a fruit fly, or a garden snail
How can such a man
Emptied of all he was
Show his worth 
In a world where he must sell
To survive

What can he sell —
When all that he is
Is a hollow shell
