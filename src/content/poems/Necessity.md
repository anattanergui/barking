# Necessity
You cannot, must not
Build anything
Upon nothing

Yet everyone tries
Never finding nothing

Emptiness
Tao, Brahman
Awareness, Turtle
Indeterminacy

Always — something
Never 
Coming from 
Nothing

Why's there something
Rather than nothing

Because something
Couldn't have
Not 
Existed

If it could have
Not existed

It would 
Be — that 
Could have not existed

Nothing forever 
Is
Something
Forever
