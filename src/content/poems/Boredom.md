# Boredom
Boredom is when
One is fed up
With being
Oneself

Utterly vacant
Absolutely pointless
Unbearably nothing

Sickness
That must move
Generate
Be seen

Boredom is
A euphemism 
For aloneness

Genesis
The first cause
