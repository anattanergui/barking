# Intent
Intention
To what does it belong
To who

Me, this little, tiny
Minuscule mind hovering 
Over, inside, around
This frame

Who or what
Is to find, the intention

These hands, eyes
Searching, not finding
Me

Not intention
But intent

That is the absence
Of me
