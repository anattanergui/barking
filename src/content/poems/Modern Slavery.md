# Modern Slavery
It would seem a waste
Of a lifetime
Of potential

To see a young person
Worshipping
Their entire lives

Someone 
Who held them
Captive —
Psychologically or emotionally 
Or under lock and key

Cut off 
From the world
Their autonomy 

A parent
Of the young person 
Witnessing their captivity 
Would raise safeguarding concerns
Involve multi-agency 
Legal teams
Wonder about capacity 
Even call it 
Modern slavery 

But who sees 
That the students
Of beloved gurus
Venerable teachers
Holy masters
Through millennia

And today 
Like those young people
Live the same lives 
That we would call
Modern slavery

Only worse 
Where the teachers 
Are the suppliers with drugs

That make students believe
That they are free
To leave
