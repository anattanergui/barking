# Schmutz
That layer of ingrained
Dust — grime —
That comes from touch
On your hands

Rubbed off
Washed away
Upon noticing

That is worn as jewels
Showcased, celebrated
Sought after

For the entirety 
Of your life

That which 
Comes upon you
Just from touch
