# Leverage
Pain
Colonises completely
Melts certainty — resistance
Controls more absolutely
Than anything else

At all costs
It seeks only
Its end

Other than how
A society’s laws prohibit
Regulate, exploit
Monetise 

Access to pain relief 
On what metric
Can any society be ranked
Civilised
Or otherwise

When controlling access
To pain relief 
Is structurally identical
To crucifixion
By another means

To see the pain
As leverage
To exert control
Is the oldest evil

The most reliable
Business model
