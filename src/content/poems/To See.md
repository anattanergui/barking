# To See
Who goes to another for answers 
I did —
But it was not the others 
I went to

It was the idea of the other
Some special other
That I had
It was to that idea
I went 

Always it was to the idea —
Of that other
Person or place
To that idea —  in me
I went 

Strange — 
That I went out
All the way around 
The world —

To see what was
In me
