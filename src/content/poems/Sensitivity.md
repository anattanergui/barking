# Sensitivity
What is
A living body 
But a bundle
Of sensitivity

Being moved 
This or that way
By sensitivity

Deed or no deed
Serving only
Sensitivity

What is ego
But an armour
Of me

Claiming ownership 
Of this bundle
Of sensitivity

Holding out
The world
From touching
Weaving
Being 

Sensitivity
