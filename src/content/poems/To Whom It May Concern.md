# To Whom It May Concern
Thank you
Even though I don’t know you —
Who — or what — do I thank, then

Can’t thank myself —
I didn’t do anything
Just happened to fumble 
Stumble 
Like a fool — a blind fool —
And somehow

Here I am
Thanking you
You —
Who I do not know

For what 
Even that — I cannot express
Cannot put in words

But there is —
Here —
This unmistakable 
Thanking —

And it’s not for me —
And yet
It can’t be
Without me —

That I could thank 
You
