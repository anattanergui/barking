# Not-Mutes
What would you call it
When two people 
Share a house, a bed
But never touch, never see
Never speak
Never share what matters 
To each of them
With one another

For years 
For decades
What would you call it

Would you call it civility
Courtesy or caring

Or call it waste
Hypocrisy or pathology

Or would you 
Like the two of them
Just not speak

Not say anything 
At all
