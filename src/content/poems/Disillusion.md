# Disillusion
Getting lost in — and
Returning from —
Thought and images
Future and past
Ideas and plots
Objects and trinkets

Trinkets
Shiny trinkets
Don’t seem very shiny
Anymore

Soft and curvaceous 
Appealing features
Don’t appeal
Anymore

This doesn’t appear
To be this 
Anymore

Here does not seem 
To be here
Anymore
