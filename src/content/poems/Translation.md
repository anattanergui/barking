# Translation
Squatting before the wall
Of a cave
Bare feet, bearded, stone in hand
An old man 

Scratches
A vertical line
And another 
Then
One horizontal line
Crossing off both 

He sits back
Looks at it
And then, around

A hint of smile 
Appears on his face
