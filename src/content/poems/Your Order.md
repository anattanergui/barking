# Your Order
How shall I spread 
How do I bend

Washed, shaved
Brushed and ready —

Scented, yielding —
Do you like to see
Me standing

Hold and press 
Touch
Look inside 

Kiss 
Lick and suck

Hear my moans
My urgent need —
Lips 
Squeezing 
Pressed 

On top
Or on my back
How would you have me

Shall I doll up 
For you —
Show how I look 

Excite without touching 
Pose and please
Be stunning —
A head-turner 
Sought after —

And then strip —
For you alone 

For all others 
Shall I be dressed 
Covered —

Only partial —
Or fully 
Hands showing —
Or hidden

And how much of the rest
Shall I reveal

How about 
Heels 
Lipstick 
Polished nails 

Would those excite them 
Shall I excite no one —
But you 

Am I yours alone —
Every hair 
Every inch of skin —
For your eyes
Your hands

Shall I be your property —
Your whore
Mother
And best friend 

Shall I be 
Exclusively yours
Bear your children —

And never question
Never want
Never ask 
A single thing
Of you
