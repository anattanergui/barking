# Decree
Seeing my true form
Bared of the scaffold
Unveiled 
Unhindered

I am beholden
To every instant
Of all ancestors’
Prayer and pain

The signatures
Of all forms
On every atom
In this body
Have decreed

The Soul
Will speak unfettered
