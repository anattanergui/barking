# Confidant
I long for a confidant who knows
My petty miseries 
Pointless struggles 
Fleeting pleasures —

Someone to stand beside me
Through sleepless nights
Of grief
The daily strife

I know —
Such a one exists nowhere
But here
Where I am 
And have always been

And yet
Sometimes —
I still wish 
That confidant 
Were someone 

But me
