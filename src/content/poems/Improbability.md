# Improbability
There’s something strange
Improbable about —
Being at ease 

Within a stretch —
Of pitch dark
Nothing to see

In utmost uncertainty
How rest —
Could come to be

Confronted with
This ungraspable 
Unknowability 

A minuscule flicker
This body —
How at home

Could it ever be

It couldn’t —

There is 
No possibility 
No probability 

Unless rest itself
Were the dark
Body —

Improbability
