# Suicide
One life ends
One consciousness — one mind —
Ends
Others do not

The line between 
The dead
And the living
Is absolute

Once crossed 
There is no return
No verdict follows
No charge remains

The one who crosses
Goes only one way
And is sealed —
Closed off from the outside
From those who remain

No authority among the living
Can enter, alter, or restore
The one who has crossed

All bonds fall away
At the edge of the line

Only the one who crosses
Stands at the boundary —
Sovereign —
For only he steps across
And closes the door behind him

Whatever reasons others may hold
Whatever meanings they ascribe
To the act of crossing —
None can reach across

Suicide, then 
Set aside from questions —
Is the definition enacted —
Not demonstration, not proof
But the revealing — the disclosure —
What one had always been

Complete
Sealed
Sovereign
