# Suicide
One life ends
One consciousness — one mind —
Ends
Others do not

The line between 
Dead
And the living
Is absolute

Once crossed 
There is no return
No verdict follows
No debt remains

The one who crosses
Goes only one way

And is sealed 
Closed off —
From the outside
From those who remain

No authority among the living
Can enter
Alter
Or restore
The one who has crossed

All bonds fall away
At the edge —
Of the line

Only the one who crosses
Stands
At the boundary 

Sovereign 
For only he steps across
And closes the door
Behind him

Whatever reasons others may hold
Whatever meanings they ascribe
To the act of crossing 
None can reach across

Suicide, then 
Set aside from questions —
Is the definition enacted —
Not demonstration
Not proof
But the revealing — the disclosure —
What one had always been

Complete
Sealed
Sovereign
