# Barking
Between 
Barking of a dog
And human speech
Sits logos

In 
Barking of a dog
And human speech
Sits psyche

Psychology
Enlisted only 
To focus  — through logos
On psyche 

Focuses instead 
On logos

Imagining it 
To be psyche 

That which — is 
Barking 

Being — logos
