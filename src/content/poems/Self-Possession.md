# Self-Possession
If self-possession means
To possess the self

Then it is 
A contradiction in terms
For there is no self to possess

Or a misnomer
For body-possession

If self-possession means

To be possessed by self
Then it is

A misnomer
For being possessed

For the body possessed
By a ghost
Demon —

Evicting intimacy —
Out of the body

To be
The self
