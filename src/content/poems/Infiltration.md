# Infiltration
Thought is the only thing
That seeks to continue

Being without form —
It infiltrates form — human —
Driving him to represent  — it —
In form

Convincing him 
That the representations 
Constitute — his continuity —

Continuity — which the human —
Could neither conceive nor desire
Had he not first
Been infiltrated by thought

Thought alone — 
Makes possible
Conception, construction 
And perception 
Of the human world

The human — could not exist —
Does not exist —
Nor does the universe
Without thought
