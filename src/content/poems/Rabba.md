# Rabba
Rabba —
Not even you 
And only you —

Exactly like me
Only, not even

Mirror 
Sound 
Silence 

Who could say
Ask —

Yet
But you
Not even —

Me —
Pulsating
Orphan

Grief, awe
Dispassion
Compulsion 

None and all
Neither and nor

Who could say
Ask —

Me, you
Not even —
