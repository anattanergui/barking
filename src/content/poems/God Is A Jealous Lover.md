# God Is A Jealous Lover

She would not tolerate
That you turn toward and face —
Even look at —
Anyone but her

She would not accept 
That you honour
Respect, seek guidance from 
Anyone but her

She would not accept 
If you penetrate, sleep with
Anyone but her —
That you give your affection
Love and attention 
To anyone but her

Whether you do or don’t —
She would have the last laugh
And would have you
All for herself
In the end
