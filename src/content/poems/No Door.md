# No Door
Withdrawal
After advance

Is that not what happens —
You advance
Knock
And as soon as the door opens
You withdraw

Still where you were
And no door

Knowing that even if
You’d entered 
You’d still be
Where you are
And no door —

So why
Go through 
Any door

And why not
When no door remains
Whether turned around
Or stepped through
