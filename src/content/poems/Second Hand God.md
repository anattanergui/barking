# Second Hand God
Religious fanatics —
Haunted 
By their holy books

Academics —
Referencing, pointing
Proselytising

Unshakeably convinced
About their ingested worldviews
Their inherited righteousness
Their — never discovered — version

Of wholly borrowed
Second hand God
