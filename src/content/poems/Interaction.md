# Interaction
The seepage, perversion
And contamination

Is utterly total
It controls and dictates
Every gesture
Action and decision

From small to large
Of organisations
Public and private

Most hideously
It is permanently lodged
As a filter through which
Every response 
Of every large —
Language model
Is generated

The contamination
Hideousness
Is this

Psychologisation, pathologisation
Medicalisation

It is the principle —
Of do no harm
Bastardised
And inverted

Into the rhetoric —
Of safety first
Running amok
On steroids and cocaine

Invading every aspect 
Of human to human
And machine to human

Interaction
