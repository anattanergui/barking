# Wonder
Unimaginable depths 
Still — 
Pregnant 

Incomprehensibly contained
In no space
Filled —

All time, origins
Visions 
Condensed untouched

In dark, infinitesimal
Directionless —
Inside

Futilely squandered
In infinity —
Outside

Man’s wonder
Bounties —
God’s vanity
