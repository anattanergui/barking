# Those Who Know: Speak
For an entire lifetime 
I wrote
Wondering 
If I might stumble 
On a true word —

A construction of sounds
To hold in my hand 
And show
As the one 
And only true word  

Having learned 
Thousands of words
And millions of sounds —
Not a single word 
I could find
That was true

Hoping no more  —
To write 
A true word 
Or convey
A true thing 

Now 
I am free 
To write 
Everything and anything
That can be written
And free to speak 
Any word

Knowing 
That all are hollow words
Empty sounds —
And no true word
Has ever been spoken
Nor can be
