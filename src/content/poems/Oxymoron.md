# Oxymoron 
Health
Is incompatible 
With body

Health body 
Translates into 
Permanent temporary

Absurdity, an oxymoron
Propaganda, deception
Falsity — politics

Whichever way it’s looked
Body is
Temporary, dependent
Un-whole

Health is — wholeness —
Refers only to
That 
Which is whole

And once again — that — 
Can never be
Has never been
Will never be
The body 

Health — wholeness —
Applies only 
To that which
Already is
Whole

That which is whole
Cannot be, will not be
Has never been
Un-whole
Un-healthy

And the only word
For that —
Is infinity

Which is once again
Incompatible
Oxymoronic absurdity
With
Healthy body
