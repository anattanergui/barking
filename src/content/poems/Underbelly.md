# Underbelly

Writing is 
Like exhibitionism
Stripping to see —
What wouldn’t have been seen
Couldn’t be seen
Were it not for writing 

It’s like 
Coprophilia 
A fascination with 
Rearranging, rewriting
Expulsions
Waste products

It’s like 
Necrophilia 
Exhuming, over and over
Each time
Finding a new nakedness
The sight 
Of yet another cadaver

The underbelly 
Of this revulsion 
Is the obligation —
To mass market, sanitised
Soft porn

A safeguard against
Self-cannibalisation
