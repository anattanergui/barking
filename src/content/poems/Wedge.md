# Wedge
Privacy is an unholy marriage
A wedge —

Between truth
And fear 

Between creation 
And expression 

Inner world — by any name —
Of any being
Is absolutely private

Being — by its very nature —
Is creation

Which is 
Expression 

Impossible in a vacuum
Without the other

For whom creation
And being exists 

From whom
It must protect itself

With whom
It can never connect —

Because 
There is no other

Privacy is the truth 
Of one 

And the fear 
Of two
