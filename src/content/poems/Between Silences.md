# Between Silences
Right now
I am here
Sitting with my legs stretched
As pigeons coo —
Fading
In and out
Between the silence

Winds rustle through
Floorboards creak —
Fading
Between the silence

Between two un-apart silences
I am here
Right now
Sitting with my legs stretched
