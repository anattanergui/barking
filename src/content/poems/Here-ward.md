# Here-ward
I keep returning
To you —
Inward
Here-ward
Now-ward
Somewhere-ward

Which direction does one go
To find you
How does one stop 
The desire —
To find you
Know you
Be you

Your desire is for you
Your love for you
Your seeking for you
You alone are

What am I 
But you
