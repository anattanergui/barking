# Asymmetrical Loss
Do you feel sad  
No longer see, hear, touch  
The one you loved  

Do you mourn  
What you buried or burned —
Or your loss
Of what could have been

Do you mourn
What’s missing in theirs —
What they could have known

Do you reckon yourself alone  
Left to hold on to
Fleeting moments
They no longer need
