# The Speaking Pond
I see —
But do I 

That there
Is reflected in me
And the reflection, I call 
That 

As if a mirror were saying 
It sees 
What’s reflected in it

That grass, tree, and person 
There —
Is not all that reflected 
In me

Just as moon 
In the sky 
Reflects in water —
In the pond

Does the pond, or water 
See
The moon 

How then can I think 
That I see
That tree
Moon
Or that 

What would one say 
To a speaking pond
Claiming it sees —
Fearing 
That it may lose
The moon 

What, but:
My dear pond —
You reflect 
Not see

As do I
This body —
This pond

Awareness is the water
The mirror 
Body the frame
That holds mirror —
The water

And 
That 
Which is out there —
Moon, tree, or person —

Can never 
Has never 
Been seen
Known
Or touched 

By the mirror 
The water
