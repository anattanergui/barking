# Is Forever
Nothing is not nothing
It is something

Nothing cannot be
It can be a word —
But more than that, it cannot be

For it to be
It has to be something
Only something can be

Words are not realities
They are just opposite concepts

Opposite concepts don’t exist —
Just like nothing

Nothing does not exist in reality
In reality, something exists

Something forever exists
Nothing forever does not exist

Something forever is
Nothing forever
Or better —
Nothing forever is
Something forever
