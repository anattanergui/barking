# At Face Value
Whatever you think 
About another 
Mother, father, sister, brother 
Friend or foe

You will never
Ever know
Despite what they say or do 

What they think 
And feel 
About you 

So take what they say
What they do
At face value
But only ever —

For the moment 
In the moment
It’s said and done 

And never 
For the moment 
That hasn’t
Yet been
