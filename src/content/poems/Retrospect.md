# Retrospect
Only a migrant
Can ever know the true worth —
Of the land, the people, and the language
He leaves behind

Because only he —
Has paid with all those things 
In full —
Only to find —
Land, people, and language

And only he —
Can ever know the true evil
The demon —
In both lands, people, and languages

Because only he 
Is both —
The possessed — to leave —
And the escapee 
To have left

And only he 
Can ever be —
The one returned —
Messiah —
Or the predator —

Or be 
The one exiled —
Parasite —
Or the prophet
