# Kun
Beyond 
The veil

There is a room
A big room
Empty 

No walls
No ceiling
No floor

Only a door

You enter
And —

The room
And the door
Disappear

You turn around 
To return

But you can’t

No more veil

Kun —

And the world
Re-appears
