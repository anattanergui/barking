# You
The Point
Of it all 
Is this

There isn’t one

None of it is
For your benefit
Nor does it benefit
The writer, speaker

None of it is
Directed toward
Pointing to
Or away from —
Anything

The Point
Of it all
Is just this

Pointing —
Nothing
Zero

You
