# Logos
Guards, watching
Washing, clothing
Nourishing —

The starved, plagued
Buried, drowned 
Condemned

From the mouths, hands
Of butchers
Rescuing 

A word 
A single, helpless
Indestructible, word

A parasite, prayer
Permeating, rooting
Pouring 

Through eyes
Hands, skin
Of butchers

Of watchers
