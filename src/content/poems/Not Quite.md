# Not Quite
Fanaa
Is not quite fanaa
But realisation, revelation
Of faani

Being the nature
Of reality

It’s not quite 
Dissolution, into
Of anyone, anything

It’s recognition, seeing
Of impermanence 
Of reality

It’s not quite 
Liberation from —
Or of, anyone

It’s knowing 
That faani 
Had no ground, no basis
In reality
