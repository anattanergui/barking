# God Speaks
Reckless destruction
Sickness, agony
And death 

Unimaginable cruelty
Corruption, slaughter
Incomprehensibility

Who do you think
Witnesses —
Stands through
It all

You
Or I

Through whose eyes
But yours
Must I see

My afflicted — wretched
Singularity

What but death
Must redeem me
Uncurse you

Of the evil
That’s me
