# Now
In reviewing tomorrow’s 
Each step as yesterday’s
Today becomes
The only new day

In viewing today’s
Each step as yesterday’s
Tomorrow becomes
The one new day

The one and only —
Tomorrow and today
Resting on yesterday —
The singular day
