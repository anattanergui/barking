# Operative
It doesn’t take long
To see 

That all mythologies
Theories, and beliefs
Are fictions

To frame fragments
Of comprehension

But distinctions matter
Between 
Immaturity
And maturity

To be
In the world
Is to live
In narratives

The journey —

That they
From definitional

Become operative
