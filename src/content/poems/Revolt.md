# Revolt
Seeing his insignificance — 
What can man do
But rebel against
Or laugh in the face of
God —
Who can strike man down
At any instant — 
Without reason
Explanation
Or notice  

Gratitude — 
For what

For this mess
Of a life

Guilt — 
For what
For being an asshole  

Fuck God
For making man
In His own image —

And then 
Shooting him down
For His entertainment
