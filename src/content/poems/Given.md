# Given
Like space and time
Like sun and stars, the wide sky, and grass
Like night and day, mountains and trees —

Like growing up and growing old
Work and play — wonder and grief
A warm bed, hot tea, dreams and sleep —

Like all that is given — 
Imagined, remembered, and named
Like the human self —
Man or woman, young or old —

God, too, is given
In countless forms, ways, and places
To us, all around

That is the gift — the curse — the price
Of being human
