# The Unnatural Recognition
For all practical purposes 
From birth till death —

To be in form 
As human or animal
Means identification

In an animal 
Or human — without language
In the absence of thought
Or without the capacity to think
About thinking —

The identification goes unrecognised
The split between 
Awareness and form
Does not exist

And — 
This, therefore is the only 
Natural state 
Of a human — as it naturally is
Of an animal

The unnatural state then
Is to recognise
Identification 

And more unnatural —
Is to seek or pursue dis-identification
From form —
Be it thought, emotion
Or identity

Suffering —
Is both a consequence 
Of the recognition of split 
And —
Mis-identification 
Of awareness with form

The end of suffering —
For all practical purposes 
For an average, ordinary human
Living in the world — as he must —

As form —
Is impossible

The only lasting reversal
Of the split — is death

Intellect —
Self-reflection, self-awareness
Mindfulness, and meditation 
Are the prices we pay
To buy suffering

All of which can only reduce
Mis-identification —
But never deliver 
Dis-identification
