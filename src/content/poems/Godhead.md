# Godhead
God — whatever name we give it —
Is necessary 
Absolutely necessary:
Psychologically, existentially
Philosophically, experientially —
Necessary —
Not essential, but necessary —

A construct —
Born of self-awareness 
Of the division it creates
Between perceiver and perceived —
Necessary for the human
For the existence, sustenance, experience
Of self-awareness itself

And yet —
The self aware being
The human
Is inescapably, irrefutably
The only means —
Through which
By which
In which
God — by whatever name —
Can ever come to exist
Can ever be known

Thus the man who bows to God
Is, in truth, bowing to himself
For in the act of worship
Man sets aside — forgets — 
Ignores and denies
His own Godhead

And if he did not
If he neither denied
Nor forgot —
He would bow
Nowhere and to no one
And yet, to everything
And everyone
