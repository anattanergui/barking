# Neti-Neti
Neti-Neti is a path —
Not the destination

We get onto the path 
By asking the question
What am I

On the path, we discard
Thoughts
Feelings
Objects of perception
All phenomena
All concepts and words
All descriptions and definitions —
Even nothing
Stillness
And silence

Every gesture
Every stance

Our answer to what am I
May be
Nothing — perceivable and conceivable —

Which means
Everything perceivable and conceivable

The question self-destructs
And we are no longer on the path
