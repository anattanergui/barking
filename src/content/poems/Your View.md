# Your View 
Has anything ever
Happened — 
A second too soon
Or an instant too late

There can only be
Three possibilities

One — things happen
Too soon, or too late
The timing got messed up
Somewhere upstream — and now
It is off, forever —
In all directions

Two — things happen
Neither early nor late
But precisely on time

But how could that be —
Unless someone, somewhere
Is holding time still —
Frozen in place

For that delay on the road
That traffic jam — 
How could it not 
Make thousands of things
Late

Three — 
Things aren’t happening 
At all

Does living — in accord
With the logical conclusion
That time is impossible
That nothing ever happens
Before or after its time —
Mean surrendering
Allowing

But do these
Not assume
Things happening
Presume the existence
Of time itself

Is surrendering, allowing
Letting things be —

Not absurdity
Resting on time

But of course —
Living, in accord
Or otherwise
Cannot happen — 
For there is no time
On which
Anything 
Could happen

Must not this lead
To the final conclusion —
That 
Nothing has ever happened
That 
Nothing 
Ever 
Is

And yet 

Reality cares not
For conclusion

And ensures
That only it —
And not what is 
Could ever

Ever
Come into 
Your view
