# Your View
Has anything ever
Happened — 
A second too soon
Or an instant too late

There can only be
Three possibilities:

One — things happen
Too soon, or too late
The timing got messed up
Somewhere upstream — and now
It is off, forever —
In all directions

Two — things happen
Neither early nor late
But precisely on time

But how could that be —
Unless someone, somewhere
Is holding time still —
Frozen in place

For that delay on the road
That traffic jam — 
How could it not 
Make thousands of things
Late

Three — 
Things aren’t happening 
At all

Is living — in accord
With the logical conclusion 
That time is impossible 
That nothing ever happens
Before or after its time —
Is that
Spiritual bypassing

Does spiritual bypassing
Not assume
Things happening
Too soon — or too late

Does it not 
Presume the existence 
Of time itself

Do those who speak
Of spiritual bypassing
Of this path 
And that one —
Not insist
On absurdity

But of course —
Living, in accord
Or otherwise
Cannot happen — 
For there is no time
On which
Anything 
Could happen

Must not this lead
To the final conclusion —
That —
Nothing has ever happened
That —
Nothing 
Ever 
Is

And yet —

Reality cares not
For conclusion

And ensures
That only it —
And not what is —
Could ever
Ever
Come into 
Your view
