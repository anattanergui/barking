# Misnomer
In the absence of a self
That could cast reflection —
What does — what can —
Self-reflection mean

If not —
This very thing
That there is no self
That reflects 

Through thoughts
Beliefs, deeds
Or form

Is self reflection then
Not a misnomer — an inversion — 
For un-self un-reflection

And being possessed 
By self — 
Thoughts, beliefs, deeds
Or form —

Is in fact, self reflection
In the only — impossible — 
Sense of the term 

Which is —
The default
Human condition
