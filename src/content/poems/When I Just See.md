# When I Just See
Why would I care to know 
The speed of light
The incalculable distance between stars
Counts of galaxies and cosmic infinity

Why would I care to see
Any spectacle on sky or lands
Marvels of past, wonders of future
Nature and shapes of species

Why would I care to hear
About virtues and sins —
Avatars, their legacies
And the fleeting span of bodies

Why would I care 
To be asked in a million ways

To reference, measure 
And look at myself 
From the outside

When I just see
That without this seeing
Nameless untouchability —

None of the above 
Or below 
Would ever — could ever 
Be
