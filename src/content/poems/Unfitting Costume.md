# Unfitting Costume
I am acting 
Wearing a costume 
That does not fit 
With badges
A name pinned on

I don’t remember 
When the play began 
Nor when this costume 
Got cast on
If it ever fitted
Or I just played along
Too long
Bored of this role —
The tedium of office work
And household chores

In still moments
I strip —
Badges and all layers
And lie down — naked
In my grave
No self, no work
No more roles to play

Like a ghost
I come back
Picking back up what I shed
Going through each day
Indifferent and aloof
To slip out again
