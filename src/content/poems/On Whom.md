# On whom
On whom
Is the joke of it all

The joke of seeking
To become, be

Finding none there
To become

None there
To even be

Finding 
And finding 
None there
To have found

And none that
Which
Is found

The joke is that

It doesn’t land

Is not a joke

But absurdity
Folly
