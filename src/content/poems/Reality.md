# Reality
If 
Any of your abstractions 
About the holiest 
And the subtlest of ideas
Do not encompass 

The children who die 
Never seeing the sky
The drooling imbeciles and aged
Rocking in nursing home
The lambs and sheep 
Slaughtered
Every second of every day

Then all your ideas 
Expressions and elaborations 
Have nothing to do 
With my reality
