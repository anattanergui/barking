# Individual Consciousness
Individual means
Individual consciousness 
Consciousness — individual consciousness
Is indivisible and bestows
Individuality and indivisibility
To the individual

Individual without consciousness
Is a corpse, and consciousness 
Without individual
Is a concept

There is no primacy
Anteriority or posteriority
Between individual and consciousness
Consciousness means
Individual consciousness 
And individual means
Individual consciousness
