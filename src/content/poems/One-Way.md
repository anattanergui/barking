# One-Way
Civility is a one-way door
That once entered
Brands shame
In the gap between skin
And the clothes

So even unclothed
And nude
One can never again
Be meaninglessly naked

In the psychic walls of the gap
Are desires structured 
Imagination inhabited —
Death planted
