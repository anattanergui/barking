# The Unshared
Who can you entrust
What’s written for yourself
Stirrings 
In the emptiness 
Like a child’s drawing —
Letters of a teen
Thoughts, feelings, private worlds
That you dare not speak —

Images within the mind —
You dare not show —
How do you convey 
Who can you entrust 
The shape of what’s underneath
The facade —

You write —
Baring yourself 
Feeling — knowing — being alone
Free to expose — 
All madness
Wretched ramblings
Plagues and scars
Landscapes — of  heart

Drawing —
The most intimate portrait
Most honest image
Of your soul —

And you know that if unshared —
Unseen by any other —
You have but only shown
Only shared — a mask

And you also know —
That the urge to expose 
To even set down on paper
Is but the need — unfulfilled —
To be seen
Wholly seen
