# Ishq
Who sits
For an hour or two
Bound —

Wide-eyed, enraptured
Hushed, absorbed 
Hanging on — every word
Taking in
Each twitch 

Spellbound — at each pause —
About to come 

Filled up
Welled inside
Right on — edge

Yes, yes —
Yes

That’s it —
Mor e —
Don’t stop —
Reveal

Your fire
And light
The flesh —
Lay bare
Your all

O’ my Lord
