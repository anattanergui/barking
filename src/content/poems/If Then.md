# If/ Then
If there’s something I cannot know
I am not omniscient

If God knows all that is
God is omniscient

If I stand apart from God
God then is not alone —
And has relationships

If no I stands outside God
We are not two, but one
What God is, I am

If there is something I cannot know
It must be illusion, not limit

What God knows, I know

The time of death cannot be known —
Therefore, it cannot exist to be known

And so, there can be no death
No time — no me
And no God
