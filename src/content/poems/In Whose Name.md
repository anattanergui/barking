# In Whose Name
What legitimises
Use of force
Violence —
By any collective

Hunger — lack
Or hunger
For more

Pack of hyenas, seeing lone calf
Circling, closing
May seem like hunger

But whose
Of pack — abstraction —
Or of each
Hyena

Nations — collectives —
Attack, violate
Erase

For the human

Or for itself
That
Does not exist
