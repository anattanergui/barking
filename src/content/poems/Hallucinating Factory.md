# Hallucinating Factory
Another carrot
Dangles over there
Not here, not now but there — 
In the empty space and time
Unseen —

Carrot dangles
And this donkey wonders
If it’s but 
A hallucination

Even here and now
In this empty space and time
Without hallucinating
The day ahead —

Train to catch
Appointments to keep
Meetings to attend
Shopping trips and cooking

What could move
This donkey 
If it didn’t see 
What isn’t there

And if both
Donkey and the carrot
Are nowhere here 
Nor there

Hallucinating factory 
Must —
The none that’s left
Be
