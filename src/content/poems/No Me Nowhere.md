# No Me Nowhere
I have looked
Up and down
Right and left
Deep inside 
And far outside  

I have looked
At night and in the day
Through a thousand winters
And in as many springs, autumns
And summers

I have looked 

Not once anywhere
Not even for an instant
Have I found a me  

So I conclude
No me is there —
Never was 
Or could be

Here or anywhere beyond
