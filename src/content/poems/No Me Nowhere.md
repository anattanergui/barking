# No Me Nowhere
I have looked
Up and down
Right and left
Deep inside 
And far outside  

I have looked
At night and in the day
Through a thousand winters
And in as many summers, autumns
And springs

I have looked —
Not once anywhere
Not even for an instant
Have I found a me  

So I conclude
No me is there —
Never was or could be
Here or anywhere beyond
